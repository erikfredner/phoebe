Phoebe's story

Our daughter Phoebe's 27 days on Earth were the most important of our lives. She was born with a complex congenital heart defect, and died from complications following the surgery she needed to repair her heart. But her story is so much more than her illness. Her short life has already saved the lives of others, and we want that to be her legacy.
