Phoebe Hunter Fredner

September 22, 2025—October 19, 2025

Phoebe's story

Our daughter Phoebe's 27 days were the most important of our lives.

Phoebe was born with a complex congenital heart defect. She died shortly after the surgery needed to repair her heart due to unforeseen complications.

But Phoebe's story is so much more than her illness. Her short life has already helped the lives of many others, and we hope that legacy will grow.

Save children with congenital heart defects

Please consider making a donation in memory of Phoebe to help other children born with congenital heart defects. About 1% of babies are born with heart defects.

As of this writing, donations in Phoebe's name have already helped at least fourteen other children with congenital heart defects get the surgeries they need. Thank you to everyone who has already donated in her name.

Heart to Heart Global Cardiac Care develops new self-sustaining regional children's heart centers where thousands of children born with heart defects can undergo life-saving treatment around the world.
