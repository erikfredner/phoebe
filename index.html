Phoebe Hunter Fredner

September 22, 2025—October 19, 2025

Phoebe's story

Our daughter Phoebe's 27 days were the most important of our lives.

Phoebe was born with a complex congenital heart defect. Due to unforeseen complications, she died shortly after the surgery needed to repair her heart.

But Phoebe's story is about so much more than her illness. Although her life was much too short, her legacy will be long.

As of this writing, donations in Phoebe's name have already helped at least fourteen other children with congenital heart defects get the surgeries they need.

Help children with heart defects

About 1% of babies are born with heart defects.

Please consider donating to help them in memory of Phoebe Hunter Fredner.

Heart to Heart Global Cardiac Care develops new self-sustaining regional children's heart centers where thousands of children born with heart defects can undergo life-saving treatment around the world.

Thank you to everyone who has donated in her memory.
