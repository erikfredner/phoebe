Phoebe's story

Our daughter Phoebe's 27 days were the most important of our lives.

Phoebe was born with a complex congenital heart defect. She died shortly after the surgery needed to repair her heart due to unforeseeable complications.

But Phoebe's story is so much more than her 27 days and her illness. Her life has already saved the lives of many others, and we hope that legacy will grow.

Save children with congenital heart defects

Please make a donation in memory of Phoebe to save other children born with congenital heart defects.

As of this writing, donations in Phoebe's name have already saved the lives of fourteen other children.

Heart to Heart Global Cardiac Care develops new self-sustaining regional children's heart centers where thousands of children born with heart defects can undergo life-saving treatment around the world.
